## Slug Construction Guide

When a university is not in this index, construct the slug from the university's full English name:

```
"University of Bristol"   →  university-of-bristol
"Durham University"       →  durham-university
"University of Warwick"   →  university-of-warwick
"SOAS University"         →  soas-university-of-london
```

Try the constructed URL. If web_fetch returns no listings, fall back to the city page.
