# FAQ Reference

Use this file when users ask about contracts, deposits, cancellation, payments, or other tenancy matters. These are general uhomes.com policies — always direct users to confirm specifics with the property or uhomes advisors.

---

## Booking & Contracts

**Q: Can I book before I get my visa / confirm my acceptance?**
A: Yes. Most uhomes.com properties allow you to hold a booking with a small deposit or even without one (look for "No Deposit" listings). If your visa is rejected or university offer is withdrawn, most providers have a cancellation clause for these circumstances — check the specific property's policy before booking.

**Q: How long is a typical contract?**
A: Most PBSA contracts are for a full academic year (44–51 weeks), aligned to the September–June academic calendar. Some properties offer shorter leases (semester or summer). Use the filter on uhomes.com to find short-lease options.

**Q: Can I cancel after booking?**
A: A cooling-off period (usually 7 days) applies after signing. Outside this window, cancellation terms vary by property. First-year students and international students denied a visa generally have stronger cancellation rights — check the individual property page on uhomes.com.

## Payments & Bills

**Q: Are bills included in the rent?**
A: Most PBSA properties on uhomes.com include all utilities (electricity, water, heating, Wi-Fi) in the weekly rent. Look for the "Bills included" or "All-inclusive" tag. Private rentals may charge separately.

**Q: Can I pay rent in instalments?**
A: Many providers offer termly payments (3 instalments per year). Some accept monthly payments. A UK guarantor may be required for flexible payment plans. Check the property page for payment options.

**Q: What currency will I pay in?**
A: You pay in the local currency of the country (GBP for UK, AUD for Australia, USD for US, etc.). uhomes.com supports payment via Alipay, WeChat Pay, international credit/debit cards, and bank transfer.

**Q: Is there a service fee?**
A: Properties tagged "No Service Fee" on uhomes.com have no extra booking charge. For others, check the property page. uhomes.com offers the same price as booking directly with the property.

## Moving In

**Q: When can I move in?**
A: Most academic-year contracts start in early-to-mid September. Exact move-in dates are shown on the property listing. Some properties offer flexible check-in on weekends.

**Q: Is the accommodation furnished?**
A: Almost all PBSA on uhomes.com is fully furnished: bed, desk, chair, wardrobe, kitchen appliances. Check the property description to confirm.

**Q: Can I do a viewing before booking?**
A: uhomes.com offers VR tours for many properties (look for "VR Viewing" tag). In-person viewings can be arranged through uhomes advisors for properties in key cities.
